# Day 10 - Trapping Rain Water

## 📝 Problem
Calculate the total amount of trapped rainwater.

## 💡 Approach
- Used Two Pointer technique.
- Maintained leftMax and rightMax.
- Water level depends on the smaller of the two maximum boundaries.

## ⏱️ Time Complexity
O(n)

## 💾 Space Complexity
O(1)

## 🧠 Pattern
Two Pointer

## 🚀 Key Learning
- Water is determined by leftMax and rightMax, not the current height.
- Compare boundaries instead of current walls.
